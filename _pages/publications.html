I actively contribute to the field of AI4Geoscience by designing and deploying machine learning solutions for complex data environments like seismic and DAS. My work addresses practical needs in subsurface data processing, interpretation, inversion, modeling and geophysical monitoring. This research, which integrates Earth Science with computational methods, has been published in journals such as Geophysics, IEEE TIP, Communications Engineering, IEEE TGRS and Computers & Geosciences.
