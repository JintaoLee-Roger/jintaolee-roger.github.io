I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at Zhejiang University, working with Prof. [Weichang Li](https://person.zju.edu.cn/0025030). I received my Ph.D. in Geophysics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2025, where I worked with the [Computational Interpretation Group (CIG)](http://cig.ustc.edu.cn/) under the supervision of Prof. [Xinming Wu](https://cig.ustc.edu.cn/xinming/list.htm). Prior to that, I obtained my B.S. degree in Geophysics from USTC in 2020.

My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning and geophysics. This includes post-stack seismic data processing, interpretation, and inversion, distributed acoustic sensing (DAS) data analysis, and the development of geophysical foundation models. I also work on building efficient geophysical software tools, including 3d geophysical data visualization.


## Research Interests
- **AI for Geophysics**
- **Foundation Models for DAS and Seismic**
- **DAS data analysis** 
- **Subsea Fiber-Optic Sensing & Marine Environment Monitoring**
- **Seismic Super-Resolution**
- **Seismic Interpretation**
- **Geophysical Software**
